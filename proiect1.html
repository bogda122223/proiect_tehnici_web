Sundar Pichai

Ceo of Google

Sundar Pichai, the CEO of Google, has made some incredible changes in the company that have benefited many people around the world. Pichai has been with Google since 2004 and has also been involved in the design of their systems since its inception. He is currently responsible for overseeing nearly all of the company’s products and services. He is also responsible for developing Google’s strategy and business plan. In the year 2022, Sundar received the Padma Bhushan Award (3rd highest civilian award in India).
